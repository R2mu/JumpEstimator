Vertical Jump Height Measurement

Instructions:

  1. Enter your body weight in kg
  2. Press the START button and place the phone in your pocket or hold it firmly
  3. Stand still for 2 seconds
  4. Perform your vertical jump
  5. After landing and standing still, press the STOP button
  6. View your results below

Mouse Simulation Mode:

  1. Press START to begin simulation
  2. Move your mouse down then quickly up to simulate the countermovement and jump
  3. The faster you move the mouse upward, the higher the simulated jump
  4. Press STOP when done
